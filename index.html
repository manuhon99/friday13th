Terror and suspense abound in this 24-hour nightmare of blood.
Crystal Lake has been shuttered for over 20 years due to several
vicious and unsolved murders. The camp's new owner and seven young
counselors are readying the property for re-opening despite warnings of
a "death curse" by local residents. The curse proves true on Friday the 13th as one by one each of the counselors is stalked by a violent killer.
